### 🟢 NY SEKTION: Copilot anbefaling

**Overskrift:** Our recommended AI tool: Microsoft Copilot

**Tekst:** Copilot is Danish Agro's official AI tool. It's included in our Microsoft 365 agreement and offers enterprise-grade security for your work data.

When this Playbook mentions AI or gives examples, think Copilot first. It's the safest choice for work involving company information.

*Other AI tools (ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini) can be useful for non-sensitive tasks, but Copilot should be your default for work.*
