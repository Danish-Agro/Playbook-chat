# The Golden Rules

Five simple rules you can use every time you work with AI.

---

**Rule 1: Always review AI output** | CTA: *Learn how to review* AI is a powerful assistant, but you're the expert.

**Rule 2: Never share confidential data** | CTA: *See what's safe to share* AI tools can't always protect sensitive information — you need to.

**Rule 3: Be transparent when you use AI** | CTA: *Transparency guidelines* Honesty builds trust — with customers, colleagues and yourself.

**Rule 4: Use AI to enhance, not replace, your expertise** | CTA: *AI + You* You're the expert. AI is the assistant.

**Rule 5: When in doubt, ask** | CTA: *Get help* Questions are encouraged — uncertainty alone is risky.
