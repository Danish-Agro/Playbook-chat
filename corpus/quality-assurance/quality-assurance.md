# Quality Assurance

AI can produce impressive results fast – and confident-sounding nonsense. This section helps you catch errors before they reach your colleagues or customers.

---

## Quick quality check (30 seconds)

In a hurry? Ask yourself these three questions:

**1. Did I actually read it?** Don't skim. AI buries mistakes in otherwise good text.

**2. Is anything surprising?** Facts you didn't know, specific statistics, confident claims – these need verification.

**3. Would I put my name on this?** If not, it needs more work.
