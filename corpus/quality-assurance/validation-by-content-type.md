## Quick reference: Validation by content type

Different content needs different levels of scrutiny.

### Marketing copy / web content

**Priority checks:**

- Tone and brand voice
- No made-up product claims
- Appropriate for target audience
- Examples are relevant and accurate

**Time needed:** 2-3 minutes

### Technical documentation

**Priority checks:**

- Technical accuracy (verify with systems/experts)
- No invented terminology
- Logical sequence of steps
- Nothing that could cause user errors

**Time needed:** 5-10 minutes

### Customer communications

**Priority checks:**

- Tone is appropriate for relationship
- No confidential information
- Factual accuracy of any promises or claims
- Clear call to action

**Time needed:** 2-3 minutes

### Internal reports / analysis

**Priority checks:**

- Data accuracy
- Source verification
- Logical conclusions
- No invented statistics

**Time needed:** 5-10 minutes

### Emails (routine)

**Priority checks:**

- Tone matches relationship
- No sensitive data
- Clear and actionable
- Reads naturally

**Time needed:** 1-2 minutes
