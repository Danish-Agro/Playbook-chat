# Microsoft Copilot Setup Guide

**Get Microsoft Copilot configured for work use.**

Microsoft offers two versions of Copilot — the free version (Bing-based) and M365 Copilot (integrated with your work account). This guide covers both.

## Which Copilot are you using?

**Free Copilot (**[**copilot.microsoft.com**](http://copilot.microsoft.com)**)**

- Available to anyone with a Microsoft account
- Powered by Bing search
- Limited privacy controls
- Not recommended for sensitive work

**M365 Copilot (your Danish Agro account)**

- Integrated with Microsoft 365
- Connected to your work documents, emails, and Teams
- Enterprise-grade security
- Training is disabled by default
- Recommended for work use

## Quick setup checklist

### For M365 Copilot:

- [ ] Sign in with your Danish Agro account
- [ ] Verify you're in "Work" mode
- [ ] Test integration with your documents
- [ ] Explore app-specific features

### For Free Copilot:

- [ ] Understand the privacy limitations
- [ ] Choose appropriate conversation style
- [ ] Use only for non-sensitive tasks

## M365 Copilot Setup

### Step 1: Access M365 Copilot

1. Go to [**copilot.microsoft.com**](http://copilot.microsoft.com) or **m365.cloud.microsoft/chat**
2. Sign in with your **Danish Agro Microsoft account**
3. Verify you see "Work" mode (not "Web" mode)

**Work mode** uses your Microsoft 365 data and has enterprise security. **Web mode** is essentially the free Copilot.

### Step 2: Understand the security model

M365 Copilot has enterprise protections built in:

- ✅ Training is disabled by default — your data isn't used to train models
- ✅ Conversations are protected by Microsoft's enterprise security
- ✅ Data stays within your organisation's Microsoft 365 environment
- ✅ Copilot only accesses data you already have permission to see

**This means:** M365 Copilot is the safest option for work tasks involving company data.

### Step 3: Explore app integrations

M365 Copilot works differently depending on which Microsoft app you're using:

**In Word:**

- Summarise documents
- Draft new content
- Rewrite sections
- Ask questions about the document

**In Outlook:**

- Summarise email threads
- Draft replies
- Find information across emails
- Schedule suggestions

**In Teams:**

- Summarise meeting discussions
- Catch up on missed meetings
- Find decisions and action items
- Draft messages

**In Excel:**

- Analyse data
- Create formulas
- Generate charts
- Answer questions about your data

**In PowerPoint:**

- Create presentations from documents
- Add slides
- Summarise presentations
- Improve design

### Step 4: Test the integration

Try these prompts to verify Copilot can access your work data:

```other
Summarise my recent emails about [project name]
```

```other
What meetings do I have this week?
```

```other
Find documents related to [topic]
```

If Copilot can't find your data, check that you're in Work mode and signed in with your Danish Agro account.

## Free Copilot Setup

### Important limitations

The free version of Copilot ([copilot.microsoft.com](http://copilot.microsoft.com) without a work account) has limited privacy controls:

- ⚠️ No clear opt-out for training
- ⚠️ Conversations may be reviewed by Microsoft
- ⚠️ Not connected to your work documents

**Recommendation:** Use free Copilot only for non-sensitive tasks like general research or public information.

### Choosing a conversation style

Free Copilot offers three styles:

**Creative**

- More imaginative responses
- Good for brainstorming and ideas
- May be less accurate

**Balanced**

- Middle ground (default)
- Good for most tasks

**Precise**

- More factual and concise
- Good for research and data
- Less creative

**For work at Danish Agro:**

- Use **Precise** for factual research and data analysis
- Use **Balanced** for content creation and general tasks
- Use **Creative** for campaign ideas and brainstorming

## Custom Instructions

Unlike ChatGPT, Copilot doesn't have a dedicated Custom Instructions feature.

**Instead, you can:**

- Start each conversation with context about your role
- Be specific in your prompts about how you want responses
- Use the app-specific features (M365 Copilot adapts to the app you're in)

**Example opening:**

```other
I'm a marketing manager at an agricultural company. I need help
writing customer communications. Please use clear, professional
language appropriate for farmers and agricultural professionals.
```

## Managing conversation history

### M365 Copilot

- History is stored in your Microsoft 365 environment
- Follows Danish Agro's data retention policies
- Contact IT if you need to bulk-delete old conversations

### Free Copilot

- History is tied to your Microsoft account
- Delete individual conversations by clicking the trash icon
- Clear all history in your Microsoft account privacy settings

## Context limits

M365 Copilot can handle moderate-length conversations, roughly 8,000-32,000 words.

**When working with documents:**

- Copilot can summarise and analyse documents you have access to
- Very large documents may need to be processed in sections
- For best results, be specific about what you need

## Common issues

**"Copilot can't find my documents"**

- Make sure you're in Work mode (not Web mode)
- Verify you're signed in with your Danish Agro account
- Check that you have permission to access those documents

**"Responses don't match my company's style"**

- Provide context about your role and audience in your prompt
- Include examples of the tone you want
- Be specific about formatting preferences

**"I'm not sure if I'm using the enterprise version"**

- Check the URL: m365.cloud.microsoft/chat or [copilot.microsoft.com](http://copilot.microsoft.com)
- Look for "Work" mode indicator
- Contact IT if you're unsure

## Security reminders

Even with M365 Copilot's enterprise security:

- Don't share information you wouldn't put in an email
- Be mindful that Copilot accesses data across Microsoft 365
- Follow the Golden Rules for all AI use
- Report any concerns to IT
